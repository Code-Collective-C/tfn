Road Wallet – real-time on-road cost control

Introducing Road Wallet, our exclusive on-line function to help you plan, manage, administer and pay for your driver on-road spending!

  • Puts you in full control of where, when and how much your driver can spend on-road, removing the need to allocate cash or EFT travelling allowances.
  • Also includes real-time transaction reporting on all driver spending.

Register now to enjoy these fantastic benefits!


Road Wallet allows you to manage driver on-road spending the same way as refuelling, using an electronic wallet with preset up and authorised spending amounts for food, overnight, laundry, sanitary, etc. As in the case of refuelling, driver spending transactions are reported in real-time to your operational dashboard, eliminating the need for expense slips reconciliation. It will revolutionise the way driver on-road spending is managed in the market!
